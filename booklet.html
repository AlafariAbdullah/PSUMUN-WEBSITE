–Hend Moughrabiah, Secretary General, PSUMUN 2023

What is MUN?

Model United Nations (MUN) is a simulation of the United Nations aiming to practically inculcate diplomacy, critical thinking, and confidence. PSUMUN is a non-profit organisation affiliated to Prince Sultan University, Riyadh. Our goal is to broaden the MUN community and bring together students to discuss current global issues.

A UNique experience

PSUMUN strives to provide you with a unique, fun, and memorable MUN experience –in and out of official proceedings. PSUMUN’s goals are to promote communication, leadership, and management skills required by delegates when representing a nation as well as creation of a hub to gain new and varied perspectives on international issues.
