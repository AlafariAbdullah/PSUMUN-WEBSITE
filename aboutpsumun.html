About PSUMUN

PSUMUN

Prince Sultan University's Model United Nations Society started off back in 2016. Many of our delegations were sent out to conferences like HarvardMUN, PragueMUN, RUHMUN, among many others, earning numerous awards in all of them. However, ever since 2019, PSUMUN has sought to host its own conference at Prince Sultan University, inviting students from all Saudi to participate and discuss topics that are defining the world as we know it.
