FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What should I do after getting accepted?

As soon as you get your country assignment, delegates should start conducting their research (Topic, Countryâ€™s stance on the Topic, etc.), start writing their Position Paper and prepare speeches.

What am I getting out of it?

Participants of Model UNs usually learn how to formally speak, speak in public, learn how to research better, learn how to work in a team, learn how to be a leader and learn how to solve issues creatively, they also get more academic recognition since Model UN is a great extracurricular activity.

When does registration open?

Chair application will open on October 8th, while delegate application will be open on October 15th.

When does registration close?

Registration closes around two weeks before the conference starts. However, country allocations are processed on a first-come-first-served basis. Therefore, do not wait too long with your registration; otherwise, your preferences might already be taken.

Who can participate in PSUMUN?

PSUMUN is one of the largest Conferences of its kind in Saudi Arabia. It is held at a University and high school level. Additionally, in order to participate delegates have to be 16 years of age when the conference starts.

I cannot make it to the Conference, how do I withdraw from the competition?

Contact the PSUMUN team to explain your case.

How many members may a delegation have?

A delegation is counted as such if it contains more than or exactly two members. A maximum number is actually not set.

I am Head Delegate, how can I register the other members of my delegation?

Contact the PSUMUN team and we will assist you.

When am I/is my delegation being allocated?

Once a delegate is accepted, attached to the email will be the country allocation and the committee that the delegate was accepted to.

Is there the possibility to participate in a double delegation?

No.

Study Guides

We publish all the study guides on our website under each committee. Check it regularly when the conference approaches to see if it was published.

Position Papers

Position papers are required to be sent to your chairs via email before the designated deadline. You can find guidelines for your position paper here.

FURTHER INFORMATION

If you have any further questions or you feel that we have not informed you sufficiently, do not hesitate to contact us or send us a message through our social media platforms. We are looking forward to welcoming you at PSUMUN 2022!
