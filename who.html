What is WHO?

A specialized United Nations agency that connects nations, partners and people to promote health, keep the world safe and serve the vulnerable.

TOPIC:
The Impact of Excessive Heat on Developing Countries

Excessive heat in developing countries leads to higher rates of heat-related illnesses, strains on healthcare systems, and worsens food and water insecurity. It also impacts economic productivity, particularly in agriculture
